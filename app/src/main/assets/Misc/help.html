How to use this App

Welcome to Cocktail Cabinet, all your cocktail recepie needs and wishlist desires.

How to Navigate to Cocktails

To Navigate to a given cocktail, swipe left when in the main menu. This will bring up a list of cocktails you may view.

Tapping on a cocktail name will load up the cocktail and close the left hand menu, giving you enough room to read about this drink

The Wishlist

The Wishlist is an briliant place to view all the cocktail drinks you wish to try! To get to your wishlist simply tap on "My Wishlist" Whenever viewing a cocktail.

Viewing your Wishlist

To view your wishlist simply swipe right when in the wishlist screen.

~ Adding a Cocktail ~

Click on the Add Cocktail Button. From here, your can select which drink you want to add to your wishlist.

~ Removing a Cocktail ~

Click on the Remove Cocktail Button. From here, your can select which drink you wish to remove from your wishlist.

DO note! You must have Drinks in your wishlist before you are able to remove them.
