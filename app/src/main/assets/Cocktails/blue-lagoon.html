Blue Lagoon

Recipe

  • 1.Shake Vodka, Lemon Juice and Blue Çurocao Together
  • 2.Strain into Highball Glass
  • 3.Add Ice
  • 4.Top with Lemonade
  • 5.Garnish with a Red Cherry and Lemon Wheel

Ingredients

  • 60ml Vodka
  • 25ml Lemon Juice
  • 30ml Blue Çurocao
  • Red Cherry
  • Lemonade

Description

Blue Lagoon is a popular summer cocktail featuring blue Curaçao. It was based on the popular movie Blue Lagoon, hence the name. One variation adds a dash of lime cordial to the mix. Also known to Benjamin Penney as a "Fruit tingle" after the candy available in some countries (Australia).
