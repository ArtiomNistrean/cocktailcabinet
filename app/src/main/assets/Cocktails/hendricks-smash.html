Hendricks Smash

Recipe

  • 1.Shake Together Hendricks Gin, Eldferflower Liquor and Lime Juice
  • 2.Strain into Ice Filled Highball Glass
  • 3.Top with Tonic Water and or Lemonade
  • 4.Garnish by adding cucumber peels into the Drink

Ingredients

  • 60ml Hendricks Gin
  • 30ml Elderflower Liquor
  • 25ml Lime Juice
  • 3 Cucumber Peels
  • Tonic / Lemonade

Description

Hendrick's Gin is a brand of gin produced by William Grant & Sons in Girvan, Scotland, and launched in 1999. In addition to the traditional juniper infusion, Hendrick's uses Bulgarian rose and cucumber to add flavour. Hendrick's gin is bottled in a dark blue apothecary-style bottle. Adding Elderflower will allow for an amazing taste twist!
